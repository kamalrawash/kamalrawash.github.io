ABOUT KOT

Kot for Training and Consultation is a private institute headquartered in Kuwait. It provides high quality services in the field of human development (Training, leadership and consultation).


VISION

Pioneering and achieving excellence in training and development at the national and international levels.


THE MISSION

Our mission is to provide pioneer and excellent services in training, consultation and development for all sections of society.


OUR VALUES

Excellence—Partnership—Creativity—High Quality


OUR SPECIALITIES

-Leadership Development and Public Speaking:

Building superior skills of effective public speaking, leadership and communication to excel in any work community.


-Consultation

Providing effective consultation services in all walks of life.


-Academic Achievement

Developing core competence necessary for outstanding academic achievement.


K.O.T. EXECUTIVE AND HOME OFFICE STAFF

Dr. Mohammad Taleb Alkandari

Associate Professor Chairman of Board of Director - Doctor of Philosophy, Syracuse University - Master of Science, Syracuse University - Bachelor of Education, Kuwait - Diploma of Education, Kuwait. Dr. AlKandari has served for three consecutive years as Head of speech and debate coaching team in the Public Authority for Applied Education and Training (PAAET), competing in Kuwait Debate National Tournament for Higher Education Institutions. Over the three years PAAET Speech and Debate Team has risen to glory and won the national championship in 2016.

Yousef Al-Zowayed

Mr. Al-Zowayed has 31 years of experience in teaching in Kuwaiti public schools. He is founder and secretary of the former Kuwaiti Society for Parents of the Disabled and member and Director of the committees of Alreyadah Center and director of Leadership Development at Kuwaiti Teacher Association. He is also member of the Gulf Disability Society and recipient of several internal and external certifications in the field of disability and special needs.

Dr. Abdulaziz Alkandari

Received his PhD degree in Information System and Technology from University of Salford, UK- Master of Science-Information Management-Information Studies School-Syracuse University. During his PhD years, he served as member in the Think Lab, where he conducted extensive research on technology-enhanced learning.
